About

Motivated and detail-oriented electrochemical materials researcher with expertise spanning solid-state and gel-polymer electrolyte systems for advanced energy storage. My research focuses on ion transport, interfacial stability, and chemo-mechanical aspects, with applications to solid-state lithium metal batteries and flexible zinc-ion batteries.

I have strong experience in inorganic and polymer electrolyte design, surface and interface engineering, and operando/in-situ characterization, enabling a fundamental understanding of electrochemical–mechanical interactions and their impact on battery performance and reliability.
